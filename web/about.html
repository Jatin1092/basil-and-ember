Our Story

Built Around the Fire

Basil & Ember started as a single wood-fired grill and a herb garden on a rooftop.

From Rooftop Grill to Neighborhood Table

In 2019, chef Arjun Malhotra started grilling for friends on a rooftop with nothing but a drum grill, a herb box, and a stack of plates. What people kept coming back for wasn't a technique — it was the pairing of something green and fresh against something charred and deep.

That contrast became the whole restaurant. Every dish at Basil & Ember still has to answer the same question the rooftop grill did: what's fresh, and what's fired?

🌱

Sourcing

We buy from five family farms within 60km, and change the menu with what they bring in.

🪵

Craft

Our kitchen runs on live wood fire — no gas line touches a finished dish.

🤝

Community

Long tables, shared plates, and a room designed for staying past dessert.

"We don't cook to impress. We cook so the table stays full for another two hours."— Arjun Malhotra, Head Chef & Founder
